The Imitation Game --- 7.99€



Our best movie to introduces someone.

Biopic about the British mathematician Alan Turing,
famous for having deciphered the secret Nazi codes contained in the Enigma machine.
His invention determined the outcome of the Second World War (1939-1945) in favor of the Allies.















MR.ROBOT --- 6.99€



MR.ROBOT, my favorite series.

Robot is an American techno-thriller and drama
television series created by Sam Esmail.
It stars Rami Malek as Elliot Alderson, a computer
security engineer and hacker who suffers from dissociative identity disorder,
social anxiety disorder, and clinical depression.



















Blackhat --- 3.99€



Not good, Not bad.

A computer virus is causing several deaths in China.
Shortly after, the Chicago stock market is attacked and the world economy
goes into crisis. The computer code used in the two attacks
had previously been used by famous hacker Nicholas Hathaway
, who is now serving a prison sentence.
